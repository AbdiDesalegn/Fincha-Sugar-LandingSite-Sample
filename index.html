About Fincha Sugar Factory

Pioneering Ethiopia's sugar industry since 1998

Our History

Established in 1998 in the Finchaa Valley, Horo Guduru Welega Zone, Oromia Region. Located 350km northwest of Addis Ababa, we operate a fully irrigated sugar estate on both banks of the Finchaa River.

Production Capacity

With a design capacity to crush 12,000 tonnes of cane per day, we produce 270,000 tonnes of sugar annually and 8.1 million liters of ethanol, contributing significantly to Ethiopia's economy.

Energy Generation

Our 31-megawatt biopower cogeneration plant supplies 21MW internally and contributes 10MW to the national grid, supporting Ethiopia's energy sector.
